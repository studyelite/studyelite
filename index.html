Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers.

Is Study ELITE really free? ▼
Yes, the basic version is free forever. Currently, we are offering Elite features for free to all users during our beta phase.
Does it work without installation? ▼
Yes! You can use the fully functional web version without downloading anything. Just click "Open in Web Browser".
Are there Android/iPhone apps? ▼
We provide a direct APK for Android. For iPhone, we use PWA technology—you can add the web app to your home screen for an app-like experience.
What happens if I change devices? ▼
As long as you create an account, all your data syncs securely to the cloud and will be available on any device you log into.
Do I need an account? ▼
You can try the app without an account, but we highly recommend creating one to sync your data and ensure you don't lose progress.
How is my data protected? ▼
We prioritize user privacy. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not sell student data to third parties.
